How to Report a Bug

If you find a bug in the software, it is very important that you provide enough information that it can be duplicated on my machine. If I cannot duplicate it, then I am very unlikely to be able to fix it. To that end, please send the following information:

  • Your Name,
  • Your contact information (e.g., your email address),
  • Your computer (processor speed, amount of memory, size of hard disk, anything else you think might be helpful),
  • Your operating system (i.e., which flavor of Windows),
  • The version of OurWord you are using (Available in the Help-About dialog.)
  • A description of the bug,
  • Step-by-step instructions to produce the bug,
  • Any screen shots or sample data if you think it will be helpful.

Finding a bug is not unlike the forensic science television shows that have recently become popular. I must sift through various clues to try to find the offending line of code. The more information you can provide, the more likely I am to come up with the solution. Thank you in advance!

How to Request a Feature

I am always interested in ways in which I can improve OurWord. I am especially interested in ideas of how I can take complicated computational tasks and make them simple, so that translators can concentrate on the art of translating, rather than the mechanics of operating a computer. If you have an idea or a request, please send the following:

  • Your Name,
  • Your contact information (e.g., your email address),
  • The nature of the idea / request,
  • Any reasoning/justification as to why this feature is desirable.
