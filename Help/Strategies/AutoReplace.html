     Auto Replace

OurWord includes an AutoReplace feature, by which you can have simple substitutions occur when you type. This can be useful for typing special characters, or as a shorthand (e.g., having a few keystrokes converted into a phrase while doing a back translation.) The feature originated as an answer to a situation where a group of languages on had a few special characters, and did not wish to install KeyMan.

The AutoReplace pairs are set up in the Writing Systems. In the Tools-Configuration dialog, under the Team Settings tab, under Writing Systems, click on AutoReplace and enter the pairs you desire. Thereafter, when typing, you will see your keystrokes replaced in accordance with the pairs you have entered.

Updated as of Version 1.0.
