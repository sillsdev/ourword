     Zoom Factor

Not all users of OurWord have perfect vision, especially in countries where eye care is not readily available. For this reason OurWord provides a Zoom setting. It is available in two places:

  • In the Tools-Configure dialog, under the Options tab (this is where an Advisor might typically acess it),
  • Under the Window drop-down menu (this is where a translator might typically acess it.)

The text may be shrunk down to 60%, of magnified up to 250%.

Updated as of Version 1.0.
