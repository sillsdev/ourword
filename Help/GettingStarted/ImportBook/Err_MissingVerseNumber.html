     Import Error: Missing Verse Number

This error occurs when there is an empty verse marker (\v); that is, there is no verse number to the right of it.

An example of this error:
     

The cure is to simply add the verse number. You will probably want to look at the previous and following verse numbers to determine which number is missing.

Alternatively, you may decide that the \v does not belong there at all, in which case you should simply delete it.

After you make the correction, click on the Try Again button to instruct OurWord to resume importing the book; or alternatively you can click on Cancel to abandon the import and fix it in some other editor.
